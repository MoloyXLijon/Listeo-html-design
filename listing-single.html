Toni Jones

2 weeks ago

We enjoyed the location of our two bedroom apartment. It was central to everything! The beds were comfortable and the kitchen was spacious. Unfortunately housekeeping hadn't replaced broken bulbs, a damaged luggage stand and broken ironing board until it was pointed out to the front desk. We found glass shards in the carpet which was unsettling. We found the noise from the street very loud, less so when the windows were closed. The bathroom light was synced with an extractor fan that was noisy at night. That said, we were comfortable and could relax in our apartment. The staff were friendly and helpful. There is no on site restaurant but we found plenty close by. It was lovely to enjoy the spa and sauna at the end of a busy day. It was good value for money, initial problems not withstanding. If you want space to self cater and room to move around, this is a viable option for visitors to Sydney.
